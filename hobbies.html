ARTS

Paint is an expression!

I am a self-taught artist who started my digital art journey at 12, using ibisPaint and MedibangPaint for original illustrations and fan art. My passion for sketching began at 6, inspired by manga and anime. At 16, I chose Art as an additional subject and created a batik project for my senior high school final project. It was a challenging and memorable experience.
